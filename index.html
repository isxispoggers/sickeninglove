Lonely Afterparty

Lonely Afterparty is a 15-year-old artist hailing from the suburbs of Atlanta, Georgia. He mainly focuses on the genres of Indie, Indie Rock, Indie Pop, Alternative Rock, and R&B. Making music since he was 13, he has improved exponentially. He mainly sings about his problematic life and the struggles he faces. With his parents divorcing at a young age, along with the struggles of constant high pressure from them.
